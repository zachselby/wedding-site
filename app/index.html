Have some questions?

I’m coming from out of town. Where should I stay?
Oscar recommends staying in downtown Lexington. Check out the 21C Museum Hotel, Gratz Park Inn, Downtown Hilton, or the Hyatt Regency. Check out this Google map for some hotel options in relation to the venue.
What's the parking situation?
There should be adequate parking at The Burl. In the event of overflow, you can park just across Manchester in the gravel lot for The Breakroom, Ethereal Brewing, Fork in the Road, etc. Please see the parking lot highlighted on our hotels map.

We urge our friends from Lexington to take an Uber or Lyft and enjoy the evening responsibly.
Is there a dress code?
Wear what you would to a wedding reception. Yes, it’s in a bar, but we’re going to be getting down to some funk and soul, so bring your dancing shoes
Are kids allowed?
Sure! As long as they’re 21 or older!
What time should I arrive?
The celebration begins at 8pm and we are sure you don’t want to miss anything!
Are you going to feed me?
Celebratory cupcakes and libations will be served post haste. Once you’ve worked up and appetite from all that dancing... you’ll be rewarded with Goodfella’s Brooklyn pizza midway through the evening.
Can I bring a date?
Of course! Just be sure to RSVP above by Feb. 17th, so we know what kind of crowd to expect.
Hashtags?
